You can create a world where no youth goes unemployed. Make a change

Youth unemployment is reaching an all time high because of the large gap in skills, knowledge and exposure among the youth. At Radix Nest, we are committed to providing skills training that span vocational/artisan and soft skills and on the job training for graduates and workers alike, to ensure that they aren't lagging behind in evolution of the workplace.

Our focus is the rural areas because they are those with most of the resources and human capital that can transform cities, nations and the world at large.

Wilhemina Agbozo

Founder of Radix-Nest
