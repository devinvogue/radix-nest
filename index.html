What We Do

We are a youth led initiative and social enterprise that seeks to provide skills training, events/workshops and mentoring for the youth in rural Ghana. We are committed to growing Ghana by training the youth, building their capacity and transforming our rural communities. Through helping to add structure to the various informal sector skills and jobs that the youth in the rural area engage in small part but its significance for us to help people

WHAT Our Partakers Say

Angela is one of the tailors who graduated from our Vocational Training. After her graduation she started looking to get a salon to work in. She is now self-employed in a salon. Caroline is one of the few young women in her age group who have acquired skills that can change their lives. She says that since most families look at girls’ education as unnecessary, she was at home until she got an opportunity with Panyadoli Vocational Training Institute, and this has completely changed her life.....
Angela Khan ( Member )
